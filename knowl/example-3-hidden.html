Compute the radius of convergence of

\begin{equation*} \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{2^{n+1}}{5^n} x^n. \end{equation*}

We compute the limit

\begin{equation*} \lim_{n\to \infty}\abs{\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}} = \lim_{n\to \infty} \frac{2^{n+2}}{5^{n+1}} \cdot \frac{5^n}{2^{n + 1}} = \frac{2}{5}, \end{equation*}

and so \(r = \frac{5}{2}\text{.}\)

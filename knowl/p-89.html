At an ordinary point, we will always follow the steps in the example above.

  1. Assume that a solution of the form \(y = \ps\) exists.
  2. Plug the series into the equation to get a recurrence relation and find the coefficients in terms of \(a_0\) and \(a_1\text{.}\)
  3. Use the ratio test to find the radius of convergence (which is where out solution is valid).
  4. Verify that the solutions are linearly independent.

  1. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle e^x = 1 + x + \frac{1}{2!} x^2 + \frac{1}{3!} x^3 + \ldots = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} x^n\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \cos x = 1 - \frac{1}{2!} x^2 + \frac{1}{4!} x^4 + \ldots = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{(-1)^n}{(2n)!} x^{2n}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \sin x = x - \frac{1}{3!} x^3 + \frac{1}{5!} x^5 + \ldots = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{(-1)^n}{(2n + 1)!} x^{2n+ 1}\)

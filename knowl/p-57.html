Assume that the coefficients in the power series

\begin{equation*} f(x) = \ps \end{equation*}

satisfy the equation

\begin{equation*} \sum_{n=1}^\infty n a_n x^n - \sum_{n=0}^\infty a_n x^{n+1} = 0. \end{equation*}

Find a formula that gives any \(a_n\) in terms of \(a_0\text{.}\)
